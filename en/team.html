The team

To Eqwater and beyond...

The team, from left to right: Martin, Mathilde, Marianne and Pablo

Presentation

We are a group of 4 students from the faculté des Bioingénieurs AGRO Louvain and the Ecole Polytechnique de Louvain.
To give more practical application to our studies we decided to participate in the project course IngénieuxSud. This represents for us an exceptional opportunity to apply knowledge in the real world, that for us, until now, was chiefly theoretical. The chance to make a real difference to real people by using our skills is a huge motivation to perfect our knowledge throughout the course and beyond.
